The Problem

Sindhi Language in the Digital Age

The problem we are solving centers on the digital exclusion of over 30 million Sindhi speakers worldwide, who are currently being left behind in the AI revolution. Mainstream AI assistants' inability to understand Sindhi creates a significant Language Barrier that prevents access to voice technology and vital digital services in healthcare, education, and government. This deficiency is further highlighted by the Limited Resources available, forcing students and professionals to struggle without essential Sindhi-supported translation and communication tools. Ultimately, this lack of linguistic inclusion introduces a profound Cultural Gap, actively threatening the digital presence and long-term vitality of the Sindhi language.

This digital exclusion limits accessibility, education, and cultural preservation for the Sindhi-speaking community globally.

Our Solution

The first comprehensive AI voice assistant built specifically for Sindhi speakers. Natural, intelligent, and culturally aware.

For the First Time Ever

Sindhi speakers can interact with AI in their own language and voice. Ask questions, get answers, and engage with technology that truly understands you.
